**BRIDGING FUTURES LTD** is a not-for-profit limited company working to help build a pathway to long-term settlement in the United Kingdom for Ukrainians who arrived under the UK’s Ukraine visa schemes.

Currently, the organisation’s core activities are:

- monitoring all public statements, government publications and other references concerning the future long-term status of Ukrainians in the UK and the Ukraine schemes;
- creating a database and structuring the collected information;
- corresponding with Members of Parliament, government officials and other influential stakeholders to raise awareness, help build political will and foster links between them.
