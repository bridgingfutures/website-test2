Spain has officially introduced new rules that make it easier for Ukrainian nationals under Temporary Protection (Protección Temporal) to switch to other residence permits without leaving the country.

According to **Instruction SEM 2/2026**, published by the Spanish Ministry of Inclusion, Social Security and Migration, Ukrainians can now transition to other legal residency pathways while remaining in Spain.

---

### ✅ What's new?

**1. Switch to another residence permit without leaving Spain**  
If you meet the requirements, you can apply for another type of residence permit, including:

- Arraigo (residency based on integration);
- work residence permits;
- residence permits for family members of Spanish citizens;
- other residence permits available under Spanish immigration law.

**2. Time spent under Temporary Protection now counts**  
The period you have legally lived in Spain under Temporary Protection will now count toward:

- the residence requirement for Arraigo;
- the five-year legal residence period required to obtain long-term residence (Residencia de Larga Duración).

**3. No need to renounce Temporary Protection in advance**  
Applicants don`t need to give up their Temporary Protection status before applying. The renunciation is only required after the new residence permit has been approved and before receiving the new TIE card.

---

### 📅 Extension of Temporary Protection  
In addition, Spain has extended Temporary Protection for Ukrainian nationals until **4 March 2027**.
